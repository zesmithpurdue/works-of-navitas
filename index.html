Works of Navitas

Another electrical team member and I decided to go to a mechanical team meeting after our meeting was over. The body of the car was reaching the finalization date, so I decided to open CATIA, the modeling package the team uses, and take a look at the car. As it turns out, as an electrical engineer, I'm not very familiar with CATIA, and I could only get half the car to show up. After digging through the settings, all I could manage to do was turn the background into a picture of a kitchen counter with a bowl. So instead of finding the other half of the car, I decided to stick the half of the car I had, into the bowl. A file was uploaded to the aerodynamics folder, “Navitas in a Bowl.jpg”. My ventures into the works of Navitas had begun...
